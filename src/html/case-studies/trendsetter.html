Bringing life to ideas through collaborative working sessions

The Trendsetter team has over 15 years experience in the jewelry industry. They had identified many pain points in the journeys of both manufacturers and consumers. Using lean, rapid design cycles and workshops we created the Trendsetter concept.

FROM RAW CONCEPTS TO VISUAL DESIGN

In an all-day Workthrough session with the Trendsetter team, we flowed out the journey of key user types and pieces of jewelry themselves. Drawing insights from this collaborative design session, we created a concept web and iOS application.

FROM RAW CONCEPTS TO VISUAL DESIGN

The mobile app focused on consumers, whose needs were oriented toward rating and purchasing items. However, the needs of manufacturers and vendors were better suited to a web dashboard where they could easily review data and manage inventory.
