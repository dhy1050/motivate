DAN SENNET, VICE PRESIDENT, FINANCE

Dan Sennet is Motivate's resident Canadian, culinary enthusiast and Vice President of Finance. After spending seven years 'growing up' in the online casual gaming industry at Arkadium as the Director of Operations, Dan has recently worked at Constant Contact and Loverly in similar roles. Throughout the past decade living and working in New York, Dan has been involved in corporate expansions and contractions, fund raising and acquisitions, budgeting, forecasting and just about everything in between. Dan is extremely excited to leverage his experiences and lessons learned to help Motivate develop into the world-class agency it is destined to become.
